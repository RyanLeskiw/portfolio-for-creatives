  • About Ryan

    I’m Ryan Leskiw, a UX Designer based in North Vancouver. Currently studying Interaction Design at Capilano University, I am passionate about creating thoughtful, highly functional digital experiences.

    My Design Philosophy

    I am driven by a love for outstanding design, taking heavy inspiration from the architectural and artistic works of Arthur Erickson, Trevor Brighton, and Ryan Travis Christian. I approach my work as a risk-taker and a communicator, valuing open-mindedness and principled decision-making.

    Off The Clock

    I am an avid outdoorsman and lifelong athlete. You can usually find me mountain biking, surfing, skateboarding, or playing rugby. When it's time to slow down, I love getting lost in historical fiction, listening to music, or planning my next travel adventure.
